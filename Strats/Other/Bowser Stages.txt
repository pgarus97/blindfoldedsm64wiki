Description: Bowser 3 is unfortunately a really difficult and precise part at the end of the game. The first two throws are consistent, but rely on RNG: it is about 50% whether Bowser runs or jumps. The last throw is around a 2 frame window dependent on your exact position. This window can be further increased with the BeginnerDrag strat.
