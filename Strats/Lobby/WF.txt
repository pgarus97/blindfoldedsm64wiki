==Fall onto the Caged Island (WF Owl)==
Description: Pretty fast star that can be tricky for beginners. Important to know is that the velocity and angle the owl is grabbed is transferred to when you release, so take note of that when stratting! Also one of the few platform dependent stars, as Wii VC controller ranges are different and the owl uses full values aka turns faster.
